    CID Human Resource Management System

    Empowering Justice Through Efficient Human Resource Management: Welcome to our HRMS portal!

    About HRMS:

    Welcome to the CID Human Resource Management System (HRMS), a comprehensive platform designed to streamline and enhance the management of personnel within the Criminal Investigation Department (CID), Headquarters, JK.

    Our HRMS offers a range of features and functionalities, catering to the unique needs of a law enforcement agency. Here are some key aspects of the system:

    • Employee Management: Efficiently manage the details and records of all department employees, ensuring a centralized and organized database.
    • Section Management: Facilitate the effective administration of different sections within the department, promoting seamless coordination and communication.
    • Division Management: Streamline the management of divisions, ensuring optimal resource allocation and operational efficiency.
    • Rank Management: Keep track of employee ranks, facilitating a clear hierarchy and structure within the organization.
    • Leave Management: Our system incorporates a leave management module that considers employee rank and leave type for approval, ensuring a fair and efficient process.
    • Profile Correction Request: Employees can request corrections to their profiles, promoting accuracy and up-to-date information.
    • Feedback System: Foster communication by providing a feedback system, allowing employees to share their thoughts and suggestions.
    • Seven Types of Login Accounts: Admin, User, Section Head, Division Head, DDO, IGP, and Special DG. Each account type has specific privileges, with only the Admin having access to all functionalities.
    • Authentication and Security: Our system prioritizes authentication and security measures to safeguard sensitive information.

    Explore the power of technology in enhancing human resource management within the Crime Investigation Department(CID). Together, let's create a safer and more efficient work environment.
